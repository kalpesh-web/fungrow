How to Earn by Fungro

Fungro is an excellent platform for teens to start earning by freelancing. Here are some steps you can follow:

  1. Create an account on Fungro.
  2. Set up your profile, showcasing your skills and previous work.
  3. Browse available jobs that match your skills.
  4. Submit proposals for jobs you are interested in.
  5. Complete the jobs and get paid!

Fungro provides a safe and supportive environment for teens to develop their skills and earn money.
