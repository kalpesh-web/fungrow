How Your Child Can Earn by Freelancing on Fungro

Fungro is designed to be a safe and secure platform for teens to explore freelancing. Here's how it works:

  1. Create an Account: Help your child create an account and set up their profile.
  2. Explore Opportunities: Your child can browse and apply for jobs that match their skills.
  3. Submit Proposals: Guide your child on how to write effective proposals.
  4. Complete Tasks: Once a job is awarded, your child can complete the task and submit it for review.
  5. Receive Payment: Payments are made securely through Fungro's platform.

Fungro provides a great opportunity for teens to learn responsibility, improve their skills, and earn money in a safe environment.
